Pricesion

Pricesion is a website built using Web Scraping. Our Website displays information of products from different eCommerce websites at the same time


There are only eCommerce website where one can buy product they wish for but there are web automation tools through which some comparison sites are built.The disadvantages of the current websites :some websites are more famous, so most of the people regardless of the price purchase the product from that website itself. There are some websites where certain products are available at lower prices compared to popular websites.

So, we made our effort to solve this problem and increase the efficiency of searching the products.
